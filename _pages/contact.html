Contact us

  • GitHub

    If you want to download the source code of sensenet you can do it on GitHub. There's also a Sensenet organization where all the Sensenet related tools, components, documentation and other resources are listed.
    Feel free to submit an issue or a pull request in the related Sensenet repo, we're eager to hear your thoughts, ideas and questions.

  • Stackoverflow

    If you stucked somewhere and need help check the existing questions with the tag sensenet or ask your own questions and let the community help you.

  • Gitter

    Share your feedbacks or quick questions about SenseNet components chatting with us on Gitter

  • Twitter

    For the latest news about sensenet, follow @sensenet on Twitter.

  • For Enterprise Customers

    Enterprise customers can submit issues or question directly to our core team through support.sensenet.com.

  • Need official vendor support?

    If you need vendor support for sensenet implementation or want to get custom development please visit our website and contact us.
